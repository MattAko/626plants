FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Shipping

Where are the plants being shipped from?

We are located near Pasadena, CA!

Do you ship outside the United States?

Currently, we can only ship to locations within the United States and Guam.

How are the plants packaged? What measures are taken to secure/protect the plants?

Can I choose the shipping carrier and/or delivery method?

We currently allow our customers to select between USPS or UPS for the carriers. You can select between Overnight or Two-Day shipping for both carriers.


If you would like us to use a different shipping method or carrier, please email us a message regarding your request as soon as possible.

How long does it take to ship?

We currently have two shipping options that you can select, Overnight and Two-Day shipping. Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee that the package will be sent on time.


In order to reduce the strain on the plants, we time our delivery to the shipping carriers to right before they do last calls. By doing this, we can limit how much time the plants are kept inside a box.

I recently made a purchase, but I am going to be unable to receive the plant when its delivered. Can I request for the shipping to be delayed?

We will gladly hold on to any plants that you have purchased until you can safely receive your new plants!
